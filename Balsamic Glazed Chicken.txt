Four 6 oz boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 tsp olive oil
1/3 cup water
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
2 tsp honey
2 tbsp butter
sauteed spinach for serving

1) Mix thyme, salt and pepper in a small bowl. Pat both sides of the chicken breasts with the spice mixture. Heat a large skillet with olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken, cook for 2 to 3 minutes (or until brown). Turn and cook the other side. transfer to a plate and cover with foil.

2) Add water, vinegar, and honey to the skillet. bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil for 2 minutes or until slightly syrupy and reduced by half. Add the butter and stir until melted. Reduce the heat to medium low and return the chicken to the pan. Cook for about 3 minutes in the glaze, both sides. Serve drizzled with glaze
