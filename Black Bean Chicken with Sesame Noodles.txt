Grill Chicken


12 ounces soba (one package)
6 Tbsp mirin or rice vinegar
3 T soy sauce
2 T sesame oil
2 T brown sugar
1 T grated ginger
4 scallions, thinly sliced
a bunch of sesame seeds


Boil water. Combine everything except noodles and scallions in a bowl and whisk together. 
Once water boils, add soba noodles to water and cook to taste. 
Strain noodles, and add to bowl. 
Mix around until noodles are coated. 
Put bowl full of sauce and noodles in fridge to cool.
When everything is ready, cut chicken into strips, serve over the cold noodles. 
Add scallions on top.
