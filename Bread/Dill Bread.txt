1 package Active Dry Yeast
1/4 cup warm water (110 degrees F)
1 cup creamed cottage cheese heated to luke warm
2 T. sugar
1 T. instant Minced Dried Onion
1 T. Butter
2 tsp. Dill Seed
1 tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp Baking Soda
1 unbeaten egg
2 1/4 - 2 1/2 cups of all purpose Flour

Soften yeast in warm water for 5 min.
Combine in mixing bowl: Cottage cheese, sugar, onion, butter, dill seed, salt, baking soda, egg, and water+yeast
Add flour to form dough, beating to form a stiff elastic dough

Place in greased bowl, cover with cloth and let rise in a warm place (80-85 deg) for 50-60 min

Punch down dough and knead. Placed in 8 inch casserole bowl or soufle dish

Let rise until light
bake at 350 for 40-50 min until golden brown
brush with butter and salt
