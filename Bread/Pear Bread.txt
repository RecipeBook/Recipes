Pear Bread (makes 2 loaves)

Ingredients:
3 large eggs, room temperature
1.5 cups sugar
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 cups all purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
4 cups finely chopped peeled ripe pears (about 4 medium)
1 tsp lemoon juice
1 cup chopped walnuts

1. In a bowl, combine the eggs, sugr, oil, and vanilla. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt
2. Stir dry ingredients into egg mixture until moistened
3. Toss pears with lemon juice. Stir pears and walnuts into batter
4. Fill 2 greased 9x5" loaf pans. Bake at 350 for 55-60 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to finish cooling
