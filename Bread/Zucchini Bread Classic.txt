Classic Zucchini Bread

Ingredients:
3 cups all purpose flour
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 tbsp cinnamon
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 cups sugar
3 eggs
1 cup vegetable oil
2 sp vanilla
2 cups grated zucchini
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup semisweet chocolate chunks

1. heat oven to 350 and grease 2 pans (8.5 x 4.5). 
2. In a large bowl, combine flour, cocoa, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. mix well and set aside
3. in medium bowl, combine sugar and eggs. Beat until well blended. add oil and vanilla and beat until combined. Stir in zucchini. 
4. Add flour mixture to wet ingredients and stir until just moistened
5. add in nuts and chocolate chunks. divide and spoon batter evently into loaves
6. bake for 55 to 60 minutes or until toothpick inserted in ceneter comes out clean. cool in pans for 10 minutes and cool completely on wire racks

*if using smaller pans, start at 45 minutes and work up to not overcook them
