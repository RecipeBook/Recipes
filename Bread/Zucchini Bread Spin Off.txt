Zucchini Bread Spin Off

Ingredients: 
3 1/4 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
3 cup sugar
1 tsp nutmeg
1 cup walnuts
1/2 cup raisins, apricots, chocolate chips...
4 eggs
1 cup oil
1/3 cup water
2 cups zucchini, shredded
1 tsp lemon juice
*2 tbs orange marmalade

1. combine dry ingredients
2. combine wet ingredients 
3. mix together
4. fold in nuts, raisins, etc.
5. bake at 350 for 1 hour. makes 2 loaf pans or 5 smaller pans (or many mini breads...). if going smaller, start at 45 minutes and keep checking
