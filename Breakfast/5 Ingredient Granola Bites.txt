Granola bites

*add water and agave as necessary to make them stick*

Ingredients:
1 heaping cup of packed dates (pitted)
1/4 cup maple syrup and agave nectar or honey
1/4 cup creamy salted peanut butter or almond butter
1 cup roasted unsalted almonds (loosely chopped)
1.5 cups rolled oats
additions: chocolate chips, dried fruit, nuts, banana chips, vanilla...

Instructions:
1. toast oats and almonds (if raw) in 350 degree oven for 10-15 minutes or until slightly golden brown
2. literally just mix everything together with hands. freeze to make them hard
