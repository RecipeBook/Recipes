Ingredients
for 1 serving

40 g (.5 cup) quaker rolled oat
100 mL (3 oz) fresh pasteurized milk
50 g (2 oz) natural yogurt
½ banana, sliced
¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
15 g (1/5 cup) pecan, chopped

Preparation
Add all of the ingredients except for the banana and pecans in a clean mason jar.
Stir everything together.
Add the banana and pecans.
Put a lid on the mason jar and refrigerate overnight
Enjoy!
