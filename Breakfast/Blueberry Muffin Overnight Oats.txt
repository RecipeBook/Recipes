Ingredients
for 1 serving

40 g (.5 cup) quaker rolled oat
100 mL (3 oz) fresh pasteurized milk
50 g (2 oz) natural yogurt
70 g (3/4 cup) blueberry
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg

Preparation
Add all of the ingredients except for the blueberries in a clean mason jar.
Stir everything together.
Add the blueberries, whole.
Put a lid on the mason jar and refrigerate overnight
Enjoy!
