Ingredients
for 1 serving

40 g (.5 cup) quaker rolled oat
100 mL (3 oz) fresh pasteurized milk
50 g (2 oz) natural yogurt
1 teaspoon honey, optional
1/2 carrot, grated
15 g (1/5 cup) walnuts, chopped
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Preparation
Add all of the ingredients except for the carrot and walnut in a clean mason jar.
Stir everything together.
Add the leftover walnuts and carrots.
Put a lid on the mason jar and refrigerate overnight
Enjoy!
