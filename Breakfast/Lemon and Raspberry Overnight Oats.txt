Ingredients
for 1 serving

40 g (1/2 cup) quaker rolled oat
100 mL (3 fl oz) fresh pasteurized milk
50 g (1/4 cup) natural yogurt
1 teaspoon lemon zest
50 g (1/3 cup) raspberry

Preparation
Add all of the ingredients except for the raspberries in a clean mason jar.
Stir everything together.
Add the raspberries.
Put a lid on the mason jar and refrigerate overnight 
Enjoy!
