1 cup plus 2 tbsp gluten free rolled oats
2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp ginger powder
1/4 tsp cloves or allspice
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
2/3 cup pumpkin puree
1/3 cup unsweetened apple sauce
1/3 cup coconut beverage or almond milk
2 bsp coconut oil, melted
1 tbsp maple syrup
1 tsp vanilla extract

egg replacer: 1/3 cup water, 2 tbsp ground flax seed. mix and sit for 4 minutes to gel together

1. blend the oats in a blender until finely ground (60 seconds). add spices, salt and baking soda
2. in another bowl, whisk together pumpkin, applesauce, milk, coconut oil, maple syrup, vanilla and egg. add wet to dry and stir until combined
3. heat pan on medium and lightly oil surface
4. one pan is hot, pour 1/4 cup amounts of batter and psread into circles
5. serve with agave or maple syrup
