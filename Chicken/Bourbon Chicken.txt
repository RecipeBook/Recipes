Marinade:
1/2 cup Bourbon
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup wate
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp ground ginger
2 tbsp white wine
1 tsp sesame oil

Bourbon Chicken:
4 chicken breasts, cubed. or boneless thighs
2 tbsp olive oil

1) mix all the marinade ingredients together in a medium bowl thoroughly. add the chicken pieces to the bowl, toss them around, cover iwth plastic wrap and refrigerate for at lest 4 hours
2) in a large skillet, heat the olvie oil over medium to high heat. add the chicken pieces and brown for ten minutes. add marinade to the chicken and continue cooking unil the marinade turns into a thicker sauce and the chicken is fully cooked, about another ten minutes.
3) serve with rice. garnish with chopped scallions
