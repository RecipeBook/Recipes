1 3.5 to 4 lb whole broiler chicken
3 cups assorted vegetables such as carrots, celery, onions, parsnips, beets, or other root vegetabes
2 tbsp softened butter
2 cups chicken broth

1) rinse chicken; thoroughly pat dry inside and out with paper towels. chill, uncovered, overnight in fridge
2) place rack in bottom third of the oven. preheat oven to 475 degrees. Gently run your fingers between skin and breast meat. sprinkle salt and pepper over top of skin
3) tuck wings under breasts. loop a long piece of kitchen string around drumbsticks in a figure 8 pattern; pull tight. pull string up and around thighs and breast; tie at neck. place chicken, breast side up, on rack in a shallow roasting pan. toss vegetables with butter. place in pan around chicken
4) roast 15 minutes; reduce heat to 375. roast 40 to 60 minutes more or until drumsticks easily move in sockets, chicken is no longer pink inside, and thermometer (inserted into center of an inside thigh muscle) registers 175 degrees. remove chicken from oven.
5) for pan sauce, place roasting pan over burner on stovetop; stir in chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits in pan. bring to boiling; boil gently until reduced by abou thalf. serve pan sauce with chicken
