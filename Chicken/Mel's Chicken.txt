3 whole skinless boneless chicken breasts, abt 1.5 lbs
2 tbsp olive oil
3 tbsp lime juice
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp turmeric 
2 tsp chopped fresh or crushed rosemary
1 tsp garlic (minced)

cut breasts lengthwise down center, discard any excess fat and membranes
In a ziploc bag, put oil, lime juice, chili powder, turmeric, rosemary, garlic, salt and pepper
Mix well, and add chicken peices, set aside until ready to cook
Grill chicken
EAT (can serve on bed of rice if wanted)
