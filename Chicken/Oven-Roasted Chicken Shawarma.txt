Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients:
Juice from 2 lemons
1/2 cup plus 1 tbsp olive oil
6 cloves of garlic, minced
1 tbsp kosher salt
2 tsp black pepper
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp turmeric
a pinch of cinnamon
red pepper flakes to taste
2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
1 large red onion, peeled and quartered
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
1. prepare the marinade for the chicken. combine lemon juice, 1/2 cup olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, cumin, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon and red pepper flakes in a large bowl, whisk to combine. Add chicken and toss to coat. cover and store in fridge for at least an hour and up to 12 hours
2. heat oven to 425 degrees. Use remaining olive oil to grease the sheet pan. add onion to the chicken marinade, toss once to combine. Remove chicken and onions from the bag and place on the pan evenly
3. roast until brown (about 30-40 minutes). Remove from oven, allow to rest for 2 minutes, and slice into bits. Scatter parsley ove rthe top and serve with tomatoes, cucumbers, pita, white sauce, hot sauce, olives, fried eggplant, feta rice (really anything)
