Coconut Chicken-4 servings

Ingredients
2 tbsp olive oil or coconut oil
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cloves minced garlic
2 cups diced tomatoes
1 pound boneless chicken breasts, cut into strips
1 tbsp curry powder
1/3 cup coconut water
1/3 cup water
1/8 tsp cinnamon
5 fresh basil leaves, chopped for garnish
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper


1. heat oil in skillet and add onions and cook until soften. add garlic and saute for one more minute
2. add tomatoes, chicken, curry powder. cook over low heat stirring for about 10-15 minutes until chicken is thoroughly cooked and mixture is thick
3. stir coconut milk and wtaer, cook for 5 more minutes
4. top with cinnamon and garnish with basil. serve with nutty green rice
