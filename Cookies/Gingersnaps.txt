3 cups flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground cloves
1/2 cup vegetable shortening
1/2 cup granulated surgar
3/4 cup molasses
1 egg, beaten

1) preheat oven to 375 degrees
2) in a large bown, mix flour, baking soda, salt and spices
3) in a large saucepan, melt shortening; cool slightly. stir in sugar, molasses and egg. mix well
4) add 2 cups flour mix. mix well
5) turn mixture onto lightly floured surface. knead in remaining flour mixture by hand. add additional flour if necessary to make firm dough
6) roll into balls. bake for 10 to 15 minutes
