1 cup butter, softeed
1/2 cup of firmly packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups finely chopped walnuts
3 to 4 tbsps red currant jelly or raspberry jam

1) In large bowl of the electric mixer, beat butter and sugar until creamy. Separate eggs. Place whites in a small bowl, lghtly beat and set aside; then beat yolks and vanilla into butter mixture
2) In another bowl, stir together flour and salt. Gradually add to butter mixture, blending thoroughly
3) to shape and fill cookies, roll dough into balls. Dip  each ball in egg whites then roll in finely chopped walnuts to coat. place on greased baking sheets, 1 inch apart.
4) with thumb, make indent in center of each ball. Neatly fill each indent with about 1/2 tsp red jelly or jam.
5) bake in 375 degree oven for 12 to 15 minutes or until lightly browned. let cool on baking sheets for about 1 minute then transfer to cookie racks and let cool completely. store air tight. 

makes abou 3 dozen
