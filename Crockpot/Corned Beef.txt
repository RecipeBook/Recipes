4 large carrots, peeled and cut into matchstick sized pieces
10 baby red potatoes, quartered
1 onion, peeled and cut into bite sized pieces
4 cups water
1 (4lb) corned beef brisket with spice packet
6oz beer
1/2 head cabbage, coarsely chopped

1) put carrots, potatoes and onion in bottom of crock pot, pour in water, place brisket on top of veggies. pour beer over frisket. sprinkle on psices from pocket, cover, set cooker on low.
2) cook brisket for 8 hours. 1/2 hour before serving, stir in cabbage and cook another 30 minutes
