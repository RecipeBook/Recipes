Apple Cinnamon andn Oat Parfait

1 tbsp toasted maple oat mixture
1/2 tsp 100% maple syrup
1/2 cup NYS apples, diced
1/2 cup Fage 0% fat greek yogurt

1) core and dice apples
2) in a glass, add 3/4 apples, then yogurt
3) pour 1/2 tsp maple syrup
4) top with toasted maple oat mixture
5) garnish with apples and drizzle syrup to finish, if desired
