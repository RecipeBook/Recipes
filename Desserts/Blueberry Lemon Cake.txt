Blueberry Lemon Cake

2 large eggs
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp salt
2 cups flour
2 tbsp baking powder
1 lemon and zest
1 tbsp corn starch
16 oz (1 pint) blueberries*
powdered sugar

1) whisk together eggs and sugar
2) add sour cream, vegetable oil, vanilla extract and salt to the egg/sugar mixture until smooth
3) whisk together flour and baking powder, and then add to the wet mixture
4) add 1 tbsp lemon juice and 1/2 tsp zest to mixture
5) in a separate bowl, add 1/2 tbsp cornstarch to the blueberries, as well as 1 tbsp of lemon juice
6) pour 1/2 mixture to a 9inch round pan, then blueberries, then rest of cake mix. top with blueberries
7) bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes
8) let cool and top with powdered sugar

*if using frozen berries, add 1 tbsp cornstarch instead of 1/2 tbsp, and forgo the lemon juice. drain any excess liquid before adding cornstarch
