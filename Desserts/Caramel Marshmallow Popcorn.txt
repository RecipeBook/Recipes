Caramel Marshmallow Popcorn

1 package of microwavable popcorn (popped)
½ cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp light corn syrup or honey
½ tsp vanilla
12 large marshmallows

1)	Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat
2)	Add brown sugar, corn syrup, vanilla, and marshmallows
3)	Turn heat to medium and stir until marshmallows are melted and mixture is smooth
4)	Pour over popcorn
5)	Store in an airtight container
