Cinnamon honey “fried” bananas
Coconut oil cooking spray
¼ teaspoon cinnamon
1 banana
1 tablespoon honey
½ tablespoon of water

1)	Mix together the honey and water
2)	Spray pan with coconut oil spray
3)	Add bananas and flip when golden brown
4)	Add the honey and water mixture and it will sizzle
5)	Add cinnamon on top
