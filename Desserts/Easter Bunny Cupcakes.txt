make your favorite cupcake
white icing
shredded coconut
pink decorators' sugar
jelly bean nose
chewable sweet tart eyes
large marshmallows 
mini marshmallows

1) frost a cupcake with the white icing and spring on shredded cocounut fur
2) cut a large marshmallow in half widthwise. squeeze each half slightly to give it an oval shape, then decorate the sticky side of each one with pink decorators' sugar and se them in place for ears
3) add a jelly bean nose and either jelly bean or snipped sweet tart eyes, and mini marshmallows for cheeks. for a finishing touch, draw on decorator's gel pupils and/or whiskers if you like
