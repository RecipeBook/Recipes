refrigerated pie crust
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
dash of salt
9 sliced apples
	ONLY MEC: MACINTOSH, EMPIRE, CORTLANT (3 OF EACH)
2 tbsp buttah, squared

1) heat oven to 425 degrees
2) in a big bowl, mix the sugar, flour, cinnamon, and the salt. Stir in the apples. 
3) pour into a pastry lined pie plate. place buttah on top. put second pie crust on top and cover. seal and pinch the sides (flute). cut slits in top
4) cover the edge of the crust with a 3inch strip of foil, all the way around, to prevent excessive browning. remove foil during the last 15 minutes of baking
5) bake until crust is brown and juice begins to bubble through the slits, about 40-50 minutes. put on middle rack of oven or else it will get too brown
