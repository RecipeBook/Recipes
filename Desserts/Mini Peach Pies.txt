peaches
pie dough
butter
sugar

1) cut a 5 inch round of refrigerated pie dough and press into a muffin pan cup.
2) place a pitted half peach into a muffin cup; top with a thin slice of butter and princle with sugar. top with a 3/5 inch round of dough, then crimp the top and bottom crust together. 
3) pierce top with a knife, brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with sugar ad bake at 350 degree until golden, about 35 minutes
