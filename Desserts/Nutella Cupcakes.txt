Nutella Fudge Cupcakes
Yields 12 brownies/muffins

Ingredients:
1/2 cup Nutella spread
1 large egg
5 tbsp unbleached all-purpose flour
1/4 cup chopped hazelnuts

1. Preheat oven to 350. Line 12 cup mini muffin tin with paper or foil liners
2. In a medium bowl, whisk the nutella and egg until smoth and well blended. Add the flour and whisk until blended
3. Spoon the batter into prepared muffin tins (about 3/4 full) and sprinkle with the chopped hazelnuts
4. Bake until a pick comes out with wet, gooey crumbs, 11 to 12 minutes. Set on rack to cool completely. Serve immediately or cover and store at room temperature for up to 3 days
