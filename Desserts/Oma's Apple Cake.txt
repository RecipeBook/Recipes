1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/3-1/2 cup granulated sugar  (for taste. i would do the 1/3 personally)
2 eggs
1 cup flour
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp vanilla
cinnamon
apples, peaches, or plums

1)Preheat oven to 400
2) by hand- cream the butter and sugar-then add the vanilla and eggs and mix well. stir in salt and flour until smooth. spread into a greased 9 inch square baking pan.
2) top with apple slices (or peeled sliced peaches or Italian plums). sprinkle with some sugar (1/4 cup or less) and some ground cinnamon
3) bake for 30 minutes
