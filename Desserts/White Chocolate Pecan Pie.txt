1 9 inch pie crust
2 cups pecan halves
1 1/2 cup white choclate or vanilla flavored chips
3 eggs
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup lgiht corn syrup
3 tbsp all purpose flour

1) preheat oven to 400. layer pecans and chips in a pie crust. in a large bowl, quickly hand whisk the eggs until slightly combined
2) add the brown sugar, corn syrup and flour. stir witha wooden spoon until thoroughly incoporated
3) pour over nuts and chips. bake for 40 to 45 minutes or until crust is golden brown and filing is set in center. cover with spray coated foil after 25 minutes of baking. cool completely, approximately 2 hours
