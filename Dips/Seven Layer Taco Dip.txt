1 (1oz) package of taco seasoning mix
1 (6 oz) can refried beans
1 (8oz) package of cream cheese, softened
1 (16 oz) container sour cream
1 (16oz) jar salsa
1 large tomato, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 bunch chopped green onions
1 small head iceberg lettuce, shredded
1 (6oz) can sliced black olives, drained
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

1) in a medium bowl, blend the taco seasoning mix and refried beans. spread the mixture onto a large serving platter
2) mix the sour cream and cream cheese in a medium bowl. spread on top of refried beans
3) top the layers with salsa. place a layer of tomato, green bell pepper, green onions and lettuce over the salsa. top with cheddar cheese. garnish with black olives
