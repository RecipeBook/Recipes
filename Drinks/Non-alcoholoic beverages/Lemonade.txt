Serves 2

2/3 cups water
6 tbsp sugar
1 tsp grated lemon rind
1/2 cup lemon juice
6 ice cubes

put water, sugar and grated lemon rind into a small pan and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. continue to boil, stirring, for 5 minutes
remove from heat and let cool to room temperature. stir in lemon juice and transfer to a pitcher and cover with plastic wrap. 
chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours
when ready to serve, rub a lemon onto the rim of glasses and dip in sugar, let dry. pour the lemon syrup from the fridge over ice and top with sparkling water.
