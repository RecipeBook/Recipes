Mint and Cucumber Refresher
Serves 1

Few sprigs of mint
1 tsp powdered sugar
1 lime, juiced
1 inch piece cucumber, thinly slice
favorite sparkling water
ice cubes

chop sprigs of mint leaves and mix with sugar
rub lime around the rim of the glass and dip in sugar, leave to dry
mix the rest of the lime juice, cucumber and mint in a pitcher and chill
when ready to serve, add mixture to glass and top with ice cubes and sparkling water
