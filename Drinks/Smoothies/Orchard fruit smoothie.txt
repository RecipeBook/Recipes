Orchard Fruit Smoothie
Serves 2

1 ripe pear, peeled and cut into fourths
1 apple, peeled and cut into fourths
2 large red plums, halved and pitted
4 ripe dark plums, halved and pitted
generous 3/4 cup water

put pear, apple, plums and water into a small pan (covered) and bring to boil over medium heat slowly. take off heat and chill. 
add chilled contents to blender until smooth
