Raspberry and Strawberry smoothie
serves 2-4

1/3 cup raspberries
1/2 cup strawberries, halved
1 cup plain yogurt
1 cup milk
1 tsp almond extract (optional)
2-3 tbsp honey, to taste

press raspberries through nyon strainer into a bowl using the back of a spoon, discard the seeds
put the rasberry puree, strawberries, yogurt, milk and extract in a blender. stir in honey to taste
