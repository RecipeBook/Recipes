Blackened Tuna with Citrus Butter
serves 4

4 tuna steaks, about 1 inch thick
1 1/4 tsp cajun seasoning, divided
1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tsp orange zest
1 tbsp fresh orange juice
1 tbsp finely chopped parsley

1) rub tuna evenly with 1 tsp of the cajun seasoning; cover and refrigerate for one hour
2) coat grill rack with nonstick cooking spray. preheat to medium (300-350).
3) for the citrus butter, use the remaining 1/4 tsp cajun seasoning, garlic, orange zest, orange juice and parsley. mix well with the butter. cover and set aside
4) grill tuna, covered, for 4 to 6 minutes per side or to desired doneness. transfer to platter, top with citrus butter, and serve
