Grilled Tilapia and Mango Salsa

Ingredients:
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp minced fresh parsley
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1 (6 oz) tilapia fillets
1 large, ripe mango; diced
1/2 red bell pepper, diced
2 tbsp minced red onion
1 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
2 tbsp lime juice
1 tbsp lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. which together olive oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice, parsley, garlic, basil, 1 tsp pepper, and 1/2 tsp salt in a bowl and put into a resealable bag. Add tilapia fillets, coat with the marinade. Squeeze out excess air and seal the bag. Marinate for 1 hour
2. prepare salsa by combining mango, red bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, and jalapeno. Add lime juice and 1 tbsp lemon juice and toss well. Season to taste
3. preheat grill for medium high heat, lightly oil grate
4. remove the tilapia from the marinade and shake off excess. Discard remaining marinade. Grill fillets until fish is no longer translucent in the center and flakes easily with a fork. Serve with salsa
