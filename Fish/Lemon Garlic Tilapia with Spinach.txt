4 5oz tilapia fillets
kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
1/4 cup of all purpose flour
6 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
3/4 cup low sodium chicken broth
1/2 cup dry white wine
grated zest and juice of 1 lemon, pluse wedges for serving
1 tbsp unsalted butter
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 5oz package baby spinach

1) pat the tilapia dry iwth paper towels, then season with 1/2 tsp salt and a frew grinds of pepper. spread the flour on a plate; dredge the tilapia in the flour, tapping off the excess. heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium high heat. add the tilapia and cook until golden brown, about three minutes per side. trasfer to plate; tent with foil to keep warm. wipe out the skillet.
2) heat 3 tabsp olive oil int he skillet over medium heat. add the chicken broth, wine, and lemon zest and juice. increase the heat to high, bring to boil and cook until the liquid is reduced by half, about five minutes; season iwth salt and pepper. add butter and whisk until slightly thickened, about 1 minute; stir in the parseley
3) meanwhile, toss the spinach witht he ramining 1 tbsp olve oil and a few grinds of pepper. divide among plates, top with the fish and drizzle with some pan sauce. serve with lemon wedges
