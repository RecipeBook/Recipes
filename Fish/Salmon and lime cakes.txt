1 lb salmon fillet, skin and bones removed
1 egg white
3 tbsp rice flour
zest of lime
2 tbsp minced ginger
2 tsp wasabi paste
3 tbsp parsley or cilantro
1/4 cup lime juice
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tbsp brown suger
canola or olive oil

Chop salmon into 1/4 inch cubes
combine with egg white, rice flour, lime zest, ginger, wasabi paste and parsley
Combine lime juice, soy, sugar in small bowl and mix well
heat oil in large frying pan over med heat
to make each cake place 2 tbsp of salmon mixture into oil and cook 35 to 45 sec on each side until lightly golden
drain on towel and keep warm while cooking remaining salmon
serve with lime mixture as dipping sauce
