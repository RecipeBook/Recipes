Salmon in parsley sauce

Ingredients:
3 tbsp finely chopped flat-leaf parsley
4 tbsp capers
1 tbsp finely minced scallion
1 tsp finely minced garlic
1/2 cup olive oil
2 pounds salmon fillets, but into 6 portions (about 5 oz each)
salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp fresh lemon juice


Directions:
1. combine the parsley, capers, scallions and garlic into a bowl. Stir in half the olive oi and set aside
2. preheat broiler or grill. brush salmon with remaining oil and cook close to the heat for 2 mins on each side. remove from heat and allow to rest for two minutes
3. season salmon with salt and pepper
4. add the lemon juice to the parsley mixture, drizzle or brush this mixture over the salmon and serve
