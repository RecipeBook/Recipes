Tilapia and Creole Tartar Sauce
serves 4

1/2 cup mayo
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp whole grain mustard
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp hot sauce
5 tilapia fillets
1/4 tsp celery salt

1) coat grill rack with nonstick cooking spray. preheat grill to medium heat (350 to 400). 
2) for the tartar sauce, in a small bowl, combine mayo, garlic, lemon juice, mustard, cayenne and hot sauce. set aside
3) season fillets with celery salt and pepper to taste. grill fillets 4 to 5 minutes per side or until fish flakes easily with a fork. transfer fillets to a serving platter, and serve with tartar sauce
