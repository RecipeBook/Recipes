SUPER SECRET. Italians are very secretative with their sauce recipes. shhhh

2 cans crushed tomatoes
1 can tomato paste
3 paste cans of water
dried oregano
fresh parsley
dried bay leaf
salt
pepper
pinch of sugar

mix all together and bring to a boil. Let simmer for 2 to 3 hours on low. stir every 15 minutes. tilt lid off to side to let some steam out while simmering.

add meatballs, sausage and pork chops (after browning in garlic, onion, and olive oil in pan) to sauce. do meatballs after sausage and pork chops. make sure to get all the yummy onion and garlic into the sauce as well!
