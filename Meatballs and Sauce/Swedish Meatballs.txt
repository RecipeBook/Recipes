1 cup plain bread crumbs
1/2 cup milk
small onion, chopped
1-1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
3 to 4 gravey packets

1) Soak breadcrumbs in 1/2 cup of milk for 15 minutes. Chop onion and add to meat.
2) add nutmeg, salt, and pepper to meat.
3) when breadcrumbs are done, add them to the meat mixture
4) make small meatballs. Brown in margarine in pan. 
5) make 3 to 4 packages of brown gravy in large pot. Add meatballs to gravey and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes, or until meatballs are fully cooked
