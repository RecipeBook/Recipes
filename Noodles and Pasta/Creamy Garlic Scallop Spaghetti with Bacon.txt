Creamy Garlic Scallop Spaghetti with Bacon
*don't need to use scallops, would taste great with shrimp or chicken*

Ingredients:
3/4 pound dry spaghetti
4 slices crisp cooked bacon, chopped
1 pound medium sized scallops
salt and pepper to season
3 tbsp olive oil
6 cloves minced garlic
1/2 cup white wine
1 cup low sodium seaood or chicken stock
1 1/2 cup whipping cream/heavy cream
2 large tomatoes, center pulp removed and diced small
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1. Cook the dry spaghetti and drain
2. While pasta is cooking, crisp cook the bacon in pan and then roughly chop, set aside
3. Season scallops with salt and pepper and pan sear them in the bacon fat for about 1.5-2 minutes per side. Set aside with the bacon
4. Drain the bacon fat from the pan and add the olive oil and garlic. Cook for only a minute to slighlty soften the garlic
5. Add the white wine and stock and simmer until the liquid reduced by about 1/3. Add the craem and simmer for an additional 2 minutes
6. Season with salt and pepper if needed, then add the bacon, tomatoes and the scallops to the sauce. Simmer for a minute or two to warm the scallops through before adding the cooked pasta and parmesan cheese
7. Toss together the pasta and sauce very well before serving
