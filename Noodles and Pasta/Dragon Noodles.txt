lo mein noodles
butter
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
2 large eggs
2 Tbsp brown sugar
2 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp Sriracha
Tofu (1/2 lb)
Bean sprouts

Cilantro
Scallions

Make noodles
Stir together Sugar, soy sauce, and sriracha
Melt butter in a skillet, and add red pepper
scramble the egg in the butter and red pepper
add the tofu and bean sprouts
Add the noodles, and sauce, serve when ready.
