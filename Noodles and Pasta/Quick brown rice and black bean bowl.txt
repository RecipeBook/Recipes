4 servings, 356 calories each

ingredients
4 tsp coconut oil
2 cups chopped baby spinach
2 cups cooked brown rice
2 cups canned black beans
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp cumin
1 avocado
1 cup tomatoes

1. heat pan on medium and add coconut oil and melt. sautee spinach until wilted
2. add rice, beans, salt, garlic powder and cumin. cook until heated through
3. before serving, add avocado and tomatoes
