Rice Noodle Salad

Ingredients:

For crispy tofu and salad:
4 ounces of thin rice noodles
1 tbsp peanut oil, canola oil, or rice bran oil
2 ounces of firm tofu, sliced and blotted dry
1 small or 1/2 large red bell pepper cut into 2 inch long julienne slices
2 tbsp minced chives
4 cups slivered romain lettuce (chiffonade)
1 ounce dry-roasted peanuts

For dressing:
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
2 tbsp seasoned rice vinegar
salt to taste
1 small garlic clove, pureed
1/3 cup roasted peanut oil 
2 tbsp grapeseed oil
1/2 tsp Asian hot pepper oil


Directions
1. place rice threads in a large bowl and cover with hot water. let sit for 20 minutes while you prepare the other ingredients
2. heat a medium skillet over medium-high heat for 2 minutes and add 1 tbsp of peanut oil/canola/rice bran. when oil is hot, add the tofu and saute on both sides until golden brown. remove from eat and allow to cool. cut into matchsticks
3. bring medium pot of water to a boil. Add slat to taste if desired. Drain the noodles and add to the boiling water. Boil for 1 minute, drain, rinse briefly with cold water and cut with scissors to manageable lengths. transfer to salad bowl and add the tofu, red peper, chives, iclantro, lettuce and half the peanuts
4. which together all the dressing ingredients and pour over the noodles and toss together. sprinkle remaining peanuts on top
