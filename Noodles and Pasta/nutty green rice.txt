serving=1/2 cup at 157 calories

ingredients
1 cup basmati or brown rice
2 cup water
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup almonds
1 bunch parsley
1 clove garlic
1.5 tsp lemon juice
1.5 tsp pepper
1/2 cucumber for garnish

1. bring water to boil and add rice and salt. simmer covered for 45 minutes and do not stir again. let cool for 10 minutes uncovered
2. lend almonds, parseley, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil and pepper in food processor
3. stir once rice is cooled
4. garnish with cucumber
