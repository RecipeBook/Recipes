dough (I get the ready to roll dough because I am terribly with dough)
¼ pound of provolone cheese
1-2 big tomatoes
1 ball of mozzarella, or shredded pizza cheese/ mozzarella cheese (easier and less goey)
pesto!!!!

1) Spray baking sheet with cooking spray! I always forget 
2) Roll the dough out. place provolone on the rolled out dough. Then add pesto as a sauce, and layer with tomatoes and cheese.
3) Cook at 375 degrees for 15 minutes or until golden
