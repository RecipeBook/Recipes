Vegetable Pizza Squared
makes 10 squares

2 10-oz cans refidgerated crescent roll dough
1 8-oz package of cream cheese, softened
2/3 cups mayo
1 tsp dried dill weed
1 tsp minced onion
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 cup chopped cauliflower
1/2 cup chopped broccoli
1/2 cup chopped green bell peppers
1 8-oz packaged shredded cheddar cheese

1) preheat oven to 400 degrees
2) unroll crescent roll dough on a small baking sheet. arrange into a single layer to serve as a crust. bake in the preheated ven for 10 mminutes, or until puffed and lightly browned
3) in a medium bowl, mix cream cheese, mayo, dill weed, onion, and garlic
4) spread the cream cheese over the crescent roll dough crust. layer with cauliflower, broccoli, and green peppers. top with cheddar cheese. bake to melt the cheese
