Potato Salad (not Grandma Dee's recipe)

Ingredients:
3lbs Yukon Gold, Russet, or red potatoes; peeled and cut to cubes
2tbsp apple cider vinegar
1 1/4 cup mayo
1 tsp celery salt
1 tsp Dijon mustard
4 hard boiled eggs, diced
2 to 3 celery stalks, diced
2 to 3 carrots, diced
1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
sea salt and black pepper to taste
optional toppping: smoked paprika, Old Bay seasoning, chopped chives...

1. Place the diced potatoes in a large stockpot and add enough water so that the potatoes are covered by 1 inch of water. Cook over medium-high heat until they are soft
2. Drain potatoes in a colander and then return to stockpot. Drizzle the vinegar on top and let them sit for 20-30 minutes or until cool enough to handle
3. Whisk together the mayo, celery salt, and mustard in a medium bowl until evenly combined.
4. Once potatoes are ready to go, add in the mayo mixture, diced eggs, celery, carrots, and onions. Toss gently until evenly combined
5. Taste and season with salt and pepper, to taste
6. Transfer the potato salad to a serving bowl, cover, and refridgerate for 1-2 hours to chill

Keep for up to 3 days in the fridge
