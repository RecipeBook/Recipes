2 cups water
1/25 cups quinoa
1 tsp vinegar (Rice or balsamic)
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp lime juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 cup mint
1/2 cup basil
1/4 cup cilantro
2 cups shredded chicken breast
2 cups grapes, halved
1/2 cup sliced and toasted almonds
3-4 cups baby spinach
1/2 cup green onions, chopped
