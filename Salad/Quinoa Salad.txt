1 cup quinoa
Juice from 1 lemon
1 clove of garlic, diced
salt and pepper to taste
little olive oil
fresh parsley or cilantro to taste
any vegetable (cucumber and tomato)
any bean (garbanzo)

1) cook quinoa according to directions and letr it cool
2) while quinoa cooks, add remaining  ingredients together and let sit
3) once quinoa is cooled, mix ingredients together. serve cold.

you can top it with nuts, seeds, or dried fruit
