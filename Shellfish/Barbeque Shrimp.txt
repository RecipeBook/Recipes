Barbeque Shrimp

2 sticks of butter
1 cup vegetable oil
1 tbsp finely minced garlic
4 bay leaves, crushed fine
2 tsp dried rosemary, crushed
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tbsp hot pepper sauce
3/4 tsp fresh lemon juice
2 lb whole shrimp in the shell
lemon and green onion for garnish


1) melt butter in a sauce pan. add the oil and mix well. add all remaining ingredients (except shrimp) and cook over medium heat until the sauce begins to boil
2) reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring frequently
3) remove from heat and let rest at room temperature for 30 minutes. 
4) add the shrimp and toss to coat thoroughly. cook over medium heat for 5 to 8 minutes, or until shrimp just begins to turn pink. preheat oven to 450
5) remove frome heat and place in oven. bake for 10 minutes
6) divide shrimp into equal portions into soup bowls and ladle the sauce over each portion. garnish with lemon and green onions
