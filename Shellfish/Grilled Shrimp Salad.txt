Grilled Shrimp Salad
serves 4

24 large shrimp, peeled and deveined (about 1 1/4 pounds)
1/4 cup fresh lemon or lime juice
1 tsp lemon or lime zest
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
4 tbsp olive oil, divided
2 hearts of romaine lettuce, halved lengthwise
1/3 cup crumbled queso fresco cheese

1) thread 6 shrimp onto each of four 10 inch skewers.
2) for the marinade, in a medium bowl, combine lemon juice, lemon zest, cumin and 2 tbsp of the oil. season with salt and peper to taste. brush marinade over shrimp; coating well. drizzle remaining oil over romaine
3) coat grill rack with nonstick spray. preheat grillt o medium high (350 to 400). place shrimp and romaine on grill. grill skewers 2 to 3 minutes per side or until shrimp turns pink. grill romaine, cut side down, 2 to 4 minutes or until slightly charred and wilted
4) remove shrimp from skewers. place shrimp on a platter with romaine hearts. sprinkle evenly with cheese and serve


if using wooden skewers, soak for 30 minutes before grilling to prevent burning
