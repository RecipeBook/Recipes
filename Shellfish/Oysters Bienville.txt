Oysters Bienville
serves 4

2 tbsp butter
1/4 cup shallots
1/4 cup chopped parsley
2 tbsp flour
1 cup warm cream
1 cup bottled clam juice
2 egg yolks
1/3 cup white wine
1/2 cup chopped shrimp
1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
2 dozen oysters
1/3 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
paprika, black pepper and lemon wedges for seasoning

1) saute shallots and parsley in butter until tender. 
2) over low heat, add flour and whisk well to smooth out the lumps. slowly stir in the cream and then the liquid.
3) in a separate bowl, beat the egg yolks with the wine. add shrimp and mushrooms and stir into the sauce
4) season with peper to tast. cok over low heat until mixture thickens
5) add oysters on half shells or on a layer of crumbs and ladle sauce over oysters. sprinkle with a topping of breadcrumbs, cheese, and dust with paprika
6) shells are usually baked in a shallow pan of rock salt in a 400 degree oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until golden brown
7) serve with lemon wedges
