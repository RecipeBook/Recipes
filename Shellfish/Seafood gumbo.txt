Seafood gumbo
serves 6

2 pounds large raw shrimp
1 pint oysters
1/2 pound fresh or frozen crabmeat (not imitation)
2 tbsp oil
2 tbsp flour
2 1/2 cups okra or 1 tbsp file powder
2 medium onions, chopped
1 14-16-oz can chopped tomatoes
2 quarts water
2 bay leaves
1 tsp salt
2 garlic clobes, chopped
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

1) peel shrimp, devein and setaside
2) make a roux-in a heavy large cast iron skillet, heat the oil. add the flour and cook over medium low heat until the flour start to brown. do not burn but cook until roux is dark brown
3) add the shrimp and cook for a few minutes. set the mixture aside
4) in a separate skillet, put another 2 tbsp oil and add the sliced okra and onions. saute for a few minutes. add the tomatoes, water, bay leaves, garlic, salt and pepper. add this mixture to skillet with the roux mixture along with the shrimmp
5) cover and cook over low heat for 20 to 25 minutes. add the oysters and crabmeat, cook another 10 minutes
6) serve over rice and garnish with chopped parsley

if using file powder instead of okra, add it at very end just before serving. dont let the mixture boil after adding the file.
