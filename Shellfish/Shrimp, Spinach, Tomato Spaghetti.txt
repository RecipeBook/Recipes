Shrimp (lots and lots!)
1 package of spinach
1-2 tomatoes
splash of lemon juice
swirl of olive oil
whole wheat spaghetti
parmesan cheese
salt and pepper
2 cloves of garlic
olive oil bread


1) Make pasta
2) sautee spinach with lemon juice, garlic and olive oil. Add tomatoes, shrimp, salt and pepper when spinach begins to wilt. 
3) serve on top of spaghetti with parmesan cheese on top with bread
