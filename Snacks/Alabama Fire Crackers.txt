1 box of saltines
2/3 cup canola oil (can substitue with vegetable)
2 packets of ranch dressing mix
2 tbsp crushed red pepper flakes
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder/onion salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 gallon bag

1. add all ingredients (except crackers) and seal bag. mix
2. add crackers, seal, and turn over a few times to coat all the crackers evenly
3. lay bag flat and let sit for an hour
4. repeat until nicely coated
5. let bag sit overnight
6. eat! would probably be really good with a nice soft cheese like brie
