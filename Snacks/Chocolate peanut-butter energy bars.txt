Chocolate peanut-butter energy bars
makes 24 bars

cooking spray
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 cup packed light brown sugar
12 cup creamy or chunky peanut butter
2 large eggs
2 tbsp canola oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 cup barley flakes or rolled oats
3/4 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup dry-roasted peanuts
1 cup semisweet choclate chips, divided

1) preheat oven to 350. spray a 13x9 inch pan with cooking spray
2) in a small bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and cinnamon. in a large bowl, beat brown sugar and peanut butter until well comined. beat in eggs, oil, and vanilla. stir in flour mixture. add barley flakes or rolled oats, cranberries, peanuts and 3/4 cup chocolate chips, stirring to combine. 
3) spread evenly in a prepared pan. bake 20 to 25 minutes, until lightly browned and firm to the touch. cool completely in pan on wire rack
4) in a small bowl set in a pan of simmering water, melt remaining 1/4 cup chocolate chips, stirring uuntil smooth. with fork, drizzle chocolate over bars; refrigerate until set. cut into 24 bars
