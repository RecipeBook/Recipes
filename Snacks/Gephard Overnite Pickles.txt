1 Quart jar/ mayo jar
2 to 3 cucumbers
1 tsp dry dill weed
1/2 tsp dry dill seed
pinch celery seed
1/2 tsp kosher salt
couple red pepper flakes (optional)
1/2 cup vinegar (1/4 cup cider and 1/4 cup regular)
1/2 cup sugar
1 onion

heat vinegar and sugar in microwave for 30 seconds. should dissolve. let cool

cucumber an donion can be sliced in food processor

shove all in jar, shake every so often
