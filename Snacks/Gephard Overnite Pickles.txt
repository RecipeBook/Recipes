Overnite Pickles

1 Quart jar/ mayo jar or 4 small jars
2 to 3 cucumbers
1 tsp dry dill weed
1/2 tsp dry dill seed
pinch celery seed
1/2 tsp kosher salt
couple red pepper flakes (optional)
1/2 cup vinegar (1/4 cup cider and 1/4 cup regular)
1/2 cup sugar OR 1/2 cup liquid sugar
1 onion

Shove all in jar, shake every so often

Jar of Spices: use 2-3 tsp per jar
1/2 cup dill weed
1/3 cup dill seed
1/3 cup kosher salt
top with celery seed
