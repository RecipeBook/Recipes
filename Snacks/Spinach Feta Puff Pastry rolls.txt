Spinach Fet Puff Pastry Rolls

4 sheets puff pastry, thawed
2 1/2 cups frozen spinach
1/2 onion, finely diced
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp olive oil
1 cup ricotta
1 cup feta, crumbled
1/2 cup parsley
1/2 tsp pepper
1 egg + 1/2 tbsp water for an egg wash

1) start by finely dicing half an onion and mincing garlic
2) heat some olive oil in a pan on medium heat and saute the diced onions until it turns soft and translucent. add the garlic and suate for another minute or so
3) add the frozen spinach in the pan, mix it with the onion and garlic. cover the pan and let the spinach thaw and heat thorugh for 4 to 5 minutes. take the lid off and suate the spinach there's no more liquid in the pan. turn off the heat and set the spinach aside to cool
4) in a large bowl, mix ricotta cheese and chopped parsley. season with black pepper to taste
5) add the cooked spinach mixutre and mix well. also mix the feta to the spinach and ricotta
6) make an egg wash by whisking one egg and a little water together and se aside for later
7) put one sheet of puff pastry on parchment paper. cut the pastry along folds into three large rectangles. divide each rectangle into four quarters. repeat with second puff pastry
8) on one shet, spoon the spinach mix in the middle of each rectangle leaving about 1/2 inch on all side. place second sheet on top of spinach mixture and using your fingers press down ad seal the edges around the spinach removing big air bubbles. crimp the edges with a fork to seal it. repeat with additional sheets
9) brush the top of the pastry with egg wash. slash the rolls with a sharp knife to allow steam to escape while baking. bake at 425 for 18-22 minutes until they are golden and puffy
