1 pound ground beef chunck
1 (8oz) package shredded cheddar cheese
1 tbsp mayo
1 (1oz) package dry onion soup mix
24 (2 inch) dinner rolls, split

1) preheat oven to 350 degrees
2) in a medium bowl, thoroughly mix ground beef chunck, cheddar cheese, may and onion soup mix
3) spread even amounts of the groud beef onto the bottom half of each roll. place top halves on, forming little sandwiches. if you purchased the rolls in aluminum foil pans,you may return them tot he same pan for making. otherwise, place them on a baking sheet or baking pan. cover the pan tightly with aluminum foil.
4) bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven or until meat is cooked thoroughly
