2 cloves of garlic
1/2 red onion, diced
1 large cucumber, diced
5 roma tomatoes, diced
1 whole zucchini
2 stalks celery, diced
1 dash of salt (to taste)
1/4 gallon tomato juice
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/8 cup red wine vinegar
2 tbsp white sugar
5 dashes of hot sauce, to taste
1 dash of black pepper, to taste
2 avocados, diced
sour cream, to serve
cilantro, to serve

1) in the bowl of a food processor or in a blender, combine the minced garlic with half the red onion, half of the cucumber, half the tomato, half the zucchini, half the celery, half the tomato juice, olive oil, red wine vinegar, sugar, hot sauce and a dash of salt
2) pulse until all ingredients are blended well; mixture will have a nice speckled, colorful texture
3) pour into a large bowl and add the rest of the tomato juice, half of the remianing onion, cucumber, tomato, zucchini, and celery (reserve the rest of the diced vegetables for garnish)
4) stir mixture together and check seasonings, adding salt if needed. chill soup for at least a couple of hours
5) remove the soup from the fridge and stir. check seasonings one last time. ladle into a bowl and garnish with remianing diced vegetables, a sliver of fresh avocado, sour cream, and cilantro
