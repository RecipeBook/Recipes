it cures all!! I always make it when I feel sick and it does the trick~ Savanna

1) Fill large sauce pot 1/2 way with water
2) add to water: chicken breasts and salt
3) bring to boil. when boiling, add: 1 small onion, 3 carrots (diced), 3 stalks of celery (diced), and 3 potatoes (diced)
4) simmer for 1.5 hours
5) make in separate pot ditalini pasta
6) take chicken out and remove bones and skin, shred meat and put back in soup.
7) put pasta in a bowl and cover with the soup. keep separate until ready to refridgerate/ freeze the rest.
8) ADD PARMESAN CHEESE to top of pasta. so good!!
