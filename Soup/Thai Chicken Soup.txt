Ingredients:
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 garlic clove
2 chicken breasts
1/2 tsp ground turmeric
1/4 tsp chili powder
1 can coconut cream (just the cream on the can of coconut milk)
3 3/4 cup chicken stock
2 tbsp lemon/lime juice
2 tbsp crunchy peanut butter
4 oz thin fine egg noodles
lemongrass
jalepeno
scalions
cilantro

Directions:

In a large pot, fry the garlic in oil and then cook the chicken and spices.
In a seperate pot, heat the chicken stock and disolve the coconut cream in the hot stock
Pour the chicken stock over the cooked chicken and add lemon or lime juice, peanut butter, and noodles
Let simmer for about 15 min after adding lemongrass and cut jalepeno
Add scallions and cilantro, remove lemongrass and jalepeno. cook for another 10 min
Serve hot.

This recipe serves 3-4 (we usually double it though)
