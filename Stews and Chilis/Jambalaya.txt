Ingredients:

2 tbsp peanut oil
1 tbsp Cajun Seasoning
10 oz andouille sausage, sliced into rounds
1 lb chicken breasts, cubed
1 onion
1 green bell pepper
2 stalks celery
3 cloves garlic
1 can diced tomatoes
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tsp hot sauce (franks?)
2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 cup uncooked white rice
3 cups chicken broth

Directions:

Heat some peanut oil in pot over medium heat
season sausage and chicken pieces with cajun seasoning
saute sausage until browned, set aside
saute chicken, set aside
In the same pot, saute onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic
Stir in crushed tomatoes, season with red pepper, black pepper, salt,
				 worcestershire sauce, and hot sauce.
Stir in chicken and sausage, cook for 10 min
stir in rice and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20-25 min
or until liquid is mostly absorbed.
