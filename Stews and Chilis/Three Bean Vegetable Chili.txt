makes 6 servings (serving=1.75c, 276 calories)

Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 large onion, diced
2 carrots, diced
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 jalapeno pepper, minced
1.5 tbsp chili power
2 tsp cumin
1.5 tsp dried oregano
1 can (28oz) diced tomatoes
1 cup water or veggie stock
1 can (15 oz) black beans, kidney beans, and great northern beans, rinsed and drained
1 can corn
1/2 tsp sea salt
fresh cilantro
chopped scallions

directions
1. heat oil and add onions, carrots, bell peppers, garlic and jalepeno and cook until translucent
2. add dry spices and cook for 1 minute
3. add tomatoes, beans, water, salt and bring to boil. cook uncovered for 30 minutes
4. serve garnished with cilantro and scallions
