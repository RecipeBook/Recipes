Honey Balsamic Brussel Sprouts

Ingredients:
1.5 lb brussel sprouts
3 tbsp olive oil
3/4 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
2 tsp honey

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 425
2. line a baking sheet with aluminum foil
3. trim off outer, dry leaves, cut the bottom off and slice sprouts lengthwise
4. in a large bowl, toss burssel srpouts with 2 tbsp of olive oil, kosher salt, and pepper to coat thoroughly 
5. transfer the brussel sprouts to baking sheet and roat until tender and carmelized, about 20 minutes
6. place brussel sprouts back in bowl. add remaining tbsp olive oil, balsamic vinegar and honey, toss to coat evenly. taste and season with salt if necessary and serve
