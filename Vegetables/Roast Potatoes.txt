Ingredients:
Cubed Potatoes
Olive Oil
Cayenne
Rosemary
Thyme
Garlic

Directions:
Heat the oven to 375 ish
Mix the cayenne and garlic into the olive oil
Spread olive oil mixture on potatoes, sprinkle an appropriate amnt of rosemary and thyme ontop.
Bake for about 35-45 min, turning the potatoes occaisionally, adding more spices if needed.
