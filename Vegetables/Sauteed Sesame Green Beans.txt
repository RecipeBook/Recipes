4 servings, 83 calories per serving

ingredients
1 pound petite green beans
1 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic
1 tbsp fresh basil or 1 tsp dried
1 tbsp roasted sesame seeds
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper, or to taste

1. steam green beans for 7-8 minutes. drain and set aside
2. heat large skillet on medium. add olive oil and garlic, saute about 2-3 minutes
3. add green beans and saute until coated and heated
4. remove from heat and toss with basil, sesame seeds, salt and pepper
